## Overview

COLOCAL is a website and content-management platform built for the **COLOCAL** project at the
International Centre for Climate Change and Development (ICCCAD). COLOCAL is a South–South,
long-term capacity-building platform of Least Developed Countries (LDCs) universities — a
consortium of founding member universities co-creating knowledge for local adaptation to
climate change through education and research.

I developed the project's web application during my time as a Research Assistant on the
COLOCAL project (Jan–Aug 2025), which also involved extensive field work and coordination.

## Key Features

- **CMS-driven content:** Programmes, resource hub, news, events, and education/training
  managed through a headless **Strapi** CMS.
- **Public knowledge platform:** Clean, accessible presentation of research outputs and
  capacity-building resources for partner universities.
- **Responsive design:** Works across devices for a geographically distributed audience.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Vue
- **CMS:** Strapi (headless)
- **Deployment:** Vercel
