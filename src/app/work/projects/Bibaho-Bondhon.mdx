## Overview

Nuptial-Link is a company that develops software for blockchain-based marriage registration. We believe that blockchain technology can revolutionize the way marriage records are managed, making them more secure, efficient, and transparent. Our software provides a secure and immutable ledger for storing marriage records, which can be accessed by authorized users from anywhere in the world. We also offer a variety of features that make it easy for users to register their marriages, including online registration, e-signatures, and automatic notifications. We are committed to providing our users with the best possible experience, and we are excited to be at the forefront of this new technology.
## Key Features

- **Solana Blockchain Integration**: Utilizes Solana's Proof of History (PoH) and Proof of Stake (PoS) for secure, high-speed marriage and divorce registration.  
- **Smart Contracts for Legal Automation**: Implements Solang-based smart contracts to automate asset distribution, child custody, and financial obligations.  
- **Decentralized Data Storage**: Uses IPFS to store encrypted marriage and divorce records, ensuring data integrity and accessibility.  
- **KYC & Identity Verification**: Integrates with Porichoy.gov.bd to authenticate users through NID and biometric validation.  
- **Tamper-Proof Marriage & Divorce Certificates**: Ensures immutability and security through blockchain-based document verification.  
- **Interoperability & Global Recognition**: Facilitates cross-border validation of marriage records for international legal acceptance.  
- **Fraud Prevention & Child Marriage Control**: Automates age verification and prevents unauthorized or fraudulent marriage registrations.  
- **User-Friendly Digital Interface**: Provides a ReactJS-powered frontend for seamless user interactions and easy registration.  

## Technologies Used

- **Solana Blockchain**: Ensures transparency, immutability, and fast transaction processing.  
- **Smart Contracts (Solang)**: Automates legal processes and secures transaction data.  
- **IPFS (InterPlanetary File System)**: Provides decentralized, secure document storage.  
- **ReactJS**: Powers the frontend for an interactive and responsive user experience.  
- **Node.js**: Handles backend operations, API requests, and blockchain interactions.  
- **Porichoy.gov.bd Integration**: Facilitates KYC verification for identity authentication.  
- **End-to-End Encryption**: Protects sensitive user data against unauthorized access.
