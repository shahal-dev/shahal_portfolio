## Overview

An **Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)** system built for the International Centre for
Climate Change and Development (ICCCAD) to manage the organisation's projects and people in
one place. I developed it during my CAP-RES research internship at ICCCAD, where I learned
how to translate cross-domain organisational needs into working software.

## Key Features

- **Project management:** Projects with budgets, status tracking (planned / active /
  completed / on-hold), and assigned managers.
- **Tasks & milestones:** Task assignment and milestone tracking per project.
- **Budgeting:** Budget items and financial tracking with precise decimal handling.
- **Attendance & reports:** Staff attendance records and project reporting.
- **Role-based access:** Distinct roles for **admin**, **project officer**, and **employee**.

## Tech Stack

- **TypeScript** across the stack
- **Express** + **Node.js** backend
- **PostgreSQL** with **Drizzle ORM** and **drizzle-zod** for typed schemas/validation
- **Zod** for runtime validation
