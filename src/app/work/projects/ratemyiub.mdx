## Overview

RateMyIUB is a community platform where students at Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB)
can rate and review courses and faculty. By surfacing honest, aggregated student feedback, it
helps peers make better-informed decisions when planning their semesters.

## Key Features

- **Course & faculty reviews:** Submit and browse ratings and written reviews.
- **Aggregated insight:** See trends across reviews instead of relying on word of mouth.
- **Student accounts:** Authenticated reviews to keep feedback authentic.
- **Search & discovery:** Quickly find a course or instructor before registration.

## Tech Stack

- **TypeScript** across the stack
- Modern full-stack web framework with a relational database
