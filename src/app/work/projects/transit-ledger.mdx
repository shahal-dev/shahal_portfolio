## Overview

TransitLedger is a train ticket booking platform that lets users browse train schedules,
book tickets, and manage their bookings — with a focus on verifiable, tamper-proof ticket
issuance. It grows out of my research into *transparent document verification and ticketing
with decentralized systems*.

## Key Features

- **Authentication & accounts:** Secure sign-up, login, and booking management.
- **Schedules & routes:** Browse train schedules and routes with seat-class selection.
- **Ticket management:** View and cancel tickets; **QR codes** for fast, verifiable
  ticket checks.
- **Real-time updates:** Live seat availability and booking updates over WebSockets.
- **Admin dashboard:** Manage trains, schedules, and seat inventory.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React, TailwindCSS, Radix UI
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express
- **Database:** PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM (NeonDB)
- **Auth:** Passport.js
- **Real-time:** WebSockets
- **Deployment:** Dockerized for portable deployment
